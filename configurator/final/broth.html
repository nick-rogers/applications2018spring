SHOYU

Boiled soy sauce with vegetables and chicken bone. A classic broth, goes well with any noodle

TONGKOTSU

Tongkotsu is thick and a little sticky. It is made by boiling pork, and is milky in its coloration.

MISO

Light or dark in color, Miso broth is made by mixing other broths with tangy Miso paste.

TSUKEMEN

Strong in flavor, Tsukemen is made with dashi cooking stock, and is the strongest-tasting broth on offer.
