Noodles Broth Toppings Check Out

Choose your noodles here!

RAMEN

Made with wheat flour, salt, and water, Ramen noodles are a classic. They go well with most broths and toppings

SHIRATAKI

Thin noodles made with konjac yam. They are a low calorie alternative to other noodles, and they taste great!

SOBA

Buckwheat noodles, dark in color, Soba noodles are often served hot or cold. Go well with most broths.

UDON

Thick noodles made with wheat flour. These delicious noodles go well with darker broths.

SHOYU

Boiled soy sauce with vegetables and chicken bone. A classic broth, goes well with any noodle

TONGKOTSU

Tongkotsu is thick and a little sticky. It is made by boiling pork, and is milky in its coloration.

MISO

Light or dark in color, Miso broth is made by mixing other broths with tangy Miso paste.

TSUKEMEN

Strong in flavor, Tsukemen is made with dashi cooking stock, and is the strongest-tasting broth on offer.

NORI

Flattened red seaweed often used as a wrap for sushi. Goes well with soup as well though!

CHASHU

Marinated braised pork belly that will top off your ramen perfectly.

SHOYU TAMAGO

Egg soaked in soy sauce. A good, very tasty source of protein, complementing most any soup

KAMABOKO

Sliced cooked white fish puree, served to top off a ramen dish.
